{% blocktrans %} The CIGNo (Collaborative Interoperable Geographic Node) network aims at implementing a collaborative federated system to manage heterogeneous multimedia data (scientific and geographic, textual documents, measurements tables, programs to generate new higher level products) whose utilization can help users (stakeholders, administrators, scientists) to generate/discover knowledge on phenomena of interest.

The three starting nodes in the network are implementing at CORILA, CNR-ISMAR and Venice Lagoon Atlas
